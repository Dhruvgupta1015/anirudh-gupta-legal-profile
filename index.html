An Emerging Advocate and Legal Researcher

Anirudh Gupta is an Enrolled Advocate with the State Bar Council of Madhya Pradesh, building a legal career oriented toward litigation, the judiciary, and academic legal research.

His professional outlook is defined by a commitment to excellence, integrity, rigorous legal analysis, and the pursuit of justice through principled advocacy and problem-solving.

With a foundation in both courtroom exposure and scholarly research — spanning constitutional law, human rights, financial regulation, data privacy, and emerging questions in technology and law — his academic research is informed by practical legal exposure, while his advocacy interests are strengthened by sustained engagement with legal research.
